About me

Welcome, my name is Areski Belaid and I have been working in Telefony & Web Development since 1999. I'm originally from Belgium but have spent the last 11 years living in Barcelona.

During the past few years, I have been authoring a few popular Telefony Open Source projects and worked with several reknown companies in the VoIP industry. My most used stack as project developer is FreeSWITCH / Asterisk combined with Python & Lua, using Django or Flask for the web / APIs.

I often work on different levels of project development, from SysAdmin to building User Experience, I'm a big Docker enthusiast & use a lot of AngularJS. I enjoy working at all levels and love understanding the exact needs of the business in order to work efficiently on building solutions.

I specialize in:
